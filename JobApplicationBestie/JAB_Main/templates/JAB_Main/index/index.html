Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I have to register when email is not required?

Job application bestie is a completely free platform but it requires a user account to work. No email will be asked while registration.

Is it 100% free?

Yes, Job Application Bestie is 100% free to use.

Will I be charged for using AI features like ChatGPT?

To use the AI features, you need to add credit in your Open AI account (creator of ChatGPT) and share the API with the platform. We do not get any money when you add credits.

Can I use the platform without the AI features?

Yes, Job Application Bestie can be used without the AI features!

I forgot my username or password. How do I recover my account?

We do not ask or store user's email address and therefore cannot recover your account.
